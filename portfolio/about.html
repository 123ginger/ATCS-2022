Who am I?



Hello. My name is Bradford Tudor



I am a lacrosse player in my junior year at Menlo school



I am pursuing the oppurtunity to play college lacrosse and am using this website as a presentation of who I am and what I want to become.



I started playing lacrosse when I was in 3rd grade and immediately fell in love with the game. I have played for various club and national teams every since and have built some of the best relationships of my life through the game.



Even if your new to the game, I would highly recommend you keep with the sport as it can bring someone immense joy, lifelong friends, and amazing collegiate academic oppurtunities.
